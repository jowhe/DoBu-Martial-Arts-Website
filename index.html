Welcome!

At DoBu Martial Arts we aim to exercise more than the mind. Karate, Jiu-Jitsu and mixed martial arts can not only help your physical state, but your mental health too. We promote a healthy mind and body, and whether you're looking for something new to try, or sharpen your skills for competition-level sports, we aim to become your first choice of martial arts studio.

Our team of friendly and experienced instructors bring decades of expertise, and will help you reach your fullest potential. We welcome individuals, couples, families and groups, of all abilities and previous experience.
